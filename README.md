# ⚡ Electricity Consumption Analysis in Saudi Arabia

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes electricity consumption data in Saudi Arabia from 2019 to 2022 across four regions (Central, Western, Eastern, Southern) and four sectors (Residential, Commercial, Industrial, Governmental).

The goal is to explore consumption patterns, apply statistical methods, and derive insights for future trends and energy efficiency.

---

## 📊 Data

* Regions: Central, Western, Eastern, Southern
* Sectors: Residential, Commercial, Industrial, Governmental
* Time period: 2019 – 2022

---

## 📈 Analysis Performed

* Mean, median, mode
* Variance and standard deviation
* Correlation analysis
* Quartiles
* Hypothesis testing (t-test)

---

## 📊 Visualizations

* Histogram
* Bar chart
* Pie chart
* Line plots

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Residential sector has the highest electricity consumption
* Industrial sector is the second highest due to economic growth
* Commercial sector consumption decreased during COVID-19
* Government sector has the lowest and most stable usage

---

## 🔮 Future Expectations

* Increasing demand in residential and industrial sectors
* Population growth will drive higher consumption
* Adoption of renewable energy may reduce overall usage
* Smart technologies may improve energy efficiency

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Use energy-efficient appliances
* Reduce unnecessary electricity usage
* Adopt solar energy solutions
* Improve home insulation
* Optimize air conditioning usage
